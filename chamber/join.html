Business Description

    NP Member

  • $500 Annually
  • Participate in annual membership engagement consultation
  • Attend member networking and business events (4 attendees)
  • Inclusion in Member Directory on website
  • Receive and offer member to member discounts and services
  • Access to social media channels (up to 4)

    Bronze Member

  • $1,000 Annually
  • All benefits of NP Member plus...
  • Attend member networking and business events (4 attendees)
  • Access to social media channels (up to 6)
  • Submit to showcase magazine (1)
  • Eligible to serve on councils and committees
  • Targeted networking, access to premier lunches

    Silver Member

  • $1,500 Annually
  • All benefits of Bronze Member plus...
  • Attend member networking and business events (10 attendees)
  • Access to social media channels (up to 8)
  • Submit to showcase magazine (2)
  • Invitations to legislative events
  • Attend Board of Directors and VIP events

    Gold Member

  • $2,000 Annually
  • All benefits of Silver Member plus...
  • Attend member networking and business events (15 attendees)
  • Access to social media channels (up to 10)
  • Submit to showcase magazine (3)
  • Ongoing government relations and advocacy support
  • Eligible for nomination to Board of Directors
